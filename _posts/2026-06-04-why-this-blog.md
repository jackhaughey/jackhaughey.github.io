# Why I Started This Blog

I created this blog as a simple place to store my ongoing learning in Kubernetes, Azure, and cloud engineering. I’m always experimenting, breaking things, fixing them again, and picking up new patterns along the way — and I wanted a space where those notes could live in a clean, searchable format.

This isn’t meant to be polished documentation. It’s a working notebook: commands I use often, concepts I want to remember, troubleshooting steps, and anything else that helps me grow as an engineer. If it helps someone else too, that’s a bonus.

The goal is consistency. Small notes, written often, building into something useful over time.
