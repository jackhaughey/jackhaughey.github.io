# Why I’m Using Fedora + Ignite

Fedora’s atomic desktops give me exactly what I want: a clean, reproducible base OS that never drifts and never surprises me. Pairing it with Ignite means I can spin up lightweight VMs that feel like containers but behave like full Linux systems, perfect for testing, templating, and keeping my workflow tidy.

It’s the combo that lets me experiment freely without polluting my host, rebuild environments instantly, and keep every machine aligned with the same declarative config. Immutable base, disposable compute, zero fuss.
